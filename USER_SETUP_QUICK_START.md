After completing the setup above, here's how to log your first flight:

2. Enter trip number and aircraft tail number
3. Add a leg with departure/arrival airports
4. Tap **"Activate Trip"** to start
5. Times auto-fill as you taxi, takeoff, land, and park (if GPS enabled)
6. Tap **"End Trip"** when complete
