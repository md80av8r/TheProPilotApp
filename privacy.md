PROPILOT PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: December 8, 2024

ProPilot ("the App") is developed by Jeffrey Kadans. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

- Flight Data: Trip numbers, flight times, airports, and crew information you enter
- Location Data: Used only for GPS-based automatic time detection (takeoff/landing)
- Device Identifiers: Used for iCloud sync between your devices

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

- All flight log data is stored in your personal iCloud account
- Location data is processed locally on your device for auto-time detection
- We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties
- We do not use your data for advertising or tracking

DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored securely in Apple's iCloud using CloudKit. We do not maintain separate servers or databases containing your personal information.

DATA RETENTION

Your data remains in your iCloud account until you delete it. Deleting the app does not automatically delete your iCloud data.

YOUR RIGHTS

You can export, modify, or delete your flight data at any time within the app.

CONTACT

For privacy questions, contact: jkadans@me.com

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
