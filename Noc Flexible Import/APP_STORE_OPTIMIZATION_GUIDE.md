## **13. APP STORE SEARCH OPTIMIZATION TIPS**

### How Apple's Algorithm Works:
1. **App Name** (30 chars) - Highest weight
2. **Subtitle** (30 chars) - High weight  
3. **Keywords** (100 chars) - Medium weight
4. **Description** - Low weight (but affects conversion!)
5. **Reviews/Ratings** - Affects ranking
6. **Downloads** - Velocity matters (more = higher rank)

### Your Strategy:
1. Keep "ProPilot" name (short, memorable)
2. Add subtitle: "Pilot Logbook & Schedule"
3. Use optimized keywords
4. Get reviews (ask users, ask colleagues)
5. Drive traffic from outside App Store
